##### Description:
The project aims to develop a web application that allows users to create digital cards in the style of the 90s. The application will offer a nostalgic experience inspired by the design and aesthetics of that decade. Users will be able to access the website and, through an intuitive interface, design and customize their own digital cards.

#### Key Features:

##### 90s Visual Style: 
The user interface, graphics, typography, and colors will draw inspiration from the distinctive design of the 90s, evoking a sense of nostalgia for users.

##### Custom Card Creation: 
Users can create personalized digital cards based on their preferences. They can choose from different backgrounds, patterns, images, and colors to design their card uniquely.

##### plit Interface: 
The main page will feature a design split into two main sections. On the right side, there will be a form or set of fields that users can fill out with the information they want to include in their card (name, repository, description, etc.). On the left side, a real-time preview of the card being created will be displayed, allowing users to see how the final card will look as they enter the information.

##### Welcome Button: 
The main page will include a welcome button that will take users to the card creation section when pressed. This transition may be accompanied by animations or visual effects that enhance the retro experience.

##### Editing Features: 
Users will be able to edit card elements directly in the preview. This includes the ability to drag and drop elements, change sizes, fonts, and text colors, as well as add images and graphics.

##### Mobile Device Compatibility: 
The application will be responsive, adapting to different screen sizes and ensuring an optimal user experience on both desktop computers and mobile devices.
